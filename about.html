About us for creepy children.

In the outskirts of the quaint and cobblestone-paved village of Oldham, lies a most peculiar bakery – for creepier children and adults alike. Long ago, in 1898, Elizabeth Weatherwax, an old woman with a profound love of the macabre and supernatural, opened the bakery, determined to provide the most delicious and spooky treats in the area. The grand old bakery, now passed down from Granny Weatherwax to her five granddaughters, features an array of treats inspired by the authors of ages past. From lurking bat cookies, to lovecraftian slimes, cupcakes in the shape of ancient tombs, and sinisterly delicious birthday cakes, Creepy Cakes for Creepy Children guarantees that guests will leave with a sugar fueled excitement. The grand old building is replete with the sounds of laughter and the scrumptious aroma of freshly-baked goodies. The walls, adorned with ghoulish gargoyles, windy cobwebs, and the likeness of Edgar Allan Poe, are guaranteed to add an extra dose of chills to any children's visit. As the sun sets, the children and adults alike drift out of the bakery, pleasantly stuffed with delightful treats and memories that will last a lifetime. With a cheeky wink, the five granddaughters bid their visitors goodbye from the bakery, inviting them to return for a spooktastic time once again.

Granny Weatherwax

Owner

Welcome to the world of Granny Weatherwax, the enigmatic proprietress of the famed Creepy Cakes bakery. Granny's twisted treats are not only beloved by her ever-growing cadre of customers, but also shrouded in stories and whispers of the faintly sinister. For each cupcake or tart she crafts, an other-worldly aura cloaks her kitchen in a mysterious pall, as if the old baker has concocted some hidden and eerie secrets in her confections. For those brave enough to brave the shadows, one can find more than a just a cake at Creepy Cakes.

Eskarina Smith

Haunting Dough Mixer

Allow us to introduce you to the one and only Eskarina Smith, Haunting Dough Mixer at the ever spooky Creepy Cakes. This enigmatic figure has been creating the most fearful and delectable desserts for years. A master of macabre confections, her dark delights bring a chill to the bones of the bravest of patrons. With a penchant for the morbid and a gothic flair, Eskarina is ever the spookiest of sights in the bakery. From fang filled cupcakes to eerie éclairs, she is sure to provide you with a frightfully delightful treat.

Theodora Grimsley

Eerie Flavoring Expert

Grimly and ghoulishly, Theodora Grimsley haunts the bakery Creepy Cakes, where she works as an Eerie Flavoring Expert. With her cadaverous pallor and macabre curiosity, Theodora is a master of concocting the most diabolical and otherworldly flavors. For years she has been mixing and stirring her devilish recipes, cooking up all manner of frightful delights for the most discerning of little monsters. Her expertise in the field of culinary terror is unparalleled, and every new flavor she creates is more spine-tingling than the last. From the ghastly chocolate to the ghostly strawberry, Theodora has a flair for the supernatural, making her the perfect candidate to work in the Creepy Cakes kitchen.

Agatha Chillstone

Unnerving Cake Icer

Agatha Chillstone was a woman of many talents. She had a knack for the macabre, a skill for making the most chilling of cakes, and a flair for the Victorian era. As an Unnerving Cake Icer at the bakery Creepy Cakes, Agatha was able to combine all of her skills to create cakes that left her patrons spellbound. She crafted cakes that featured the most haunting of designs, from cobweb-covered ghosts to blood-soaked coffins. With each cake, Agatha worked her dark magic to create a chillingly delicious experience that her clients would never forget.

Rowena Wraithbourne

Menecing Bread Gluer

Rowena Wraithbourne is a terrifying and macabre figure who works as a Menacing Bread Gluer at the bakery Creepy Cakes. This peculiar place is renowned for its Victorian language and horror themes, making it the perfect haunt for Rowena to ply her trade. With a cackle of delight, she glues together the breads in shapes of skeletal hands, bats, spiders, and skulls, and always ensures the breads are of the highest quality. Her devotion to her craft has made her a favourite amongst the children who come to the bakery, with Rowena often joining them in their dark, twisted games.

Morgana Scrimshaw

Ghoulish Pastry Chef

Allow us to introduce you to Morgana Scrimshaw, Ghoulish Pastry Chef extraordinaire! Morgana has been conjuring up all manner of wicked delights at the bakery Creepy Cakes for many a year. Her macabre treats are guaranteed to send chills down your spine and a smile across your lips. From her devilishly delicious cakes to her morbidly magnificent cupcakes, Morgana's darkly delightful pastries are the talk of the town. Her creations are inspired by all manner of Victorian horror themes, so you can be sure that her delicacies are both frightfully fabulous and spookily scrumptious.
