About us

For Creepy Children

Tucked away in the village of Mortshill lies the infamous Creepy Cakes bakery. Established in 1920, by the matriarch of the Weatherwax family, Granny Weatherwax, it has been harvesting the attention of villagers and neighbouring towns alike ever since. The allure of their exquisite bakes has enchanted young and old alike - some of whom perhaps found themselves within the doors of the creaky old bakery more than once. The uncanny and beguiling Granny Weatherwax, together with her five granddaughters, creates an assortment of spooky treats that satisfies all sorts of cravings.

From eerie eye and tentacle cupcakes to blood red velvet cakes, each and every one of Granny's confections are made with passion and precision, and have all the necessary ingredients to tantalise the taste buds. These unearthly treats are no doubt the perfect choice for a child's birthday party, especially for those that like to revel in the macabre. The sweet-smelling bakery makes sure these scarifying treats are drenched in horror and glee; with a sprinkle of terror, for an added surprise. The incomparable taste, coupled with the creepier-than-life aesthetic, makes Creepy Cakes a must visit for all those in search of something a little different. Granny Weatherwax's recipes are a mix of the old and the new, with a pinch of old-fashioned terror, making sure her customers' sweet tooth and appetite for horror remain satisfied.

Granny Weatherwax

Owner

Granny Weatherwax has been a fixture of darkness since before anyone can remember. Her silhouette is carved into the shadows of her spooky bakery, Creepy Cakes - she is the unquestioned master and ruler of the dough and frosting. Granny's twisted treats are not only beloved by her ever-growing cadre of customers, but also shrouded in stories and whispers of the faintly sinister. For each cupcake or tart she crafts, an other-worldly aura cloaks her kitchen in a mysterious pall, as if the old baker has concocted some hidden and eerie secrets in her confections. For those brave enough to brave the shadows, one can find more than a just a cake at Creepy Cakes for Creepy Children.
