About us for creepy children.

The Weatherwax family had a long history of baking prowess, but no one expected the success of their Creepy Cakes bakery. It all began in 1898, when Granny Weatherwax opened the bakery with her five granddaughters in tow. The bakery became famous for its eccentric cakes, which were designed to look like monstrous creatures and mythical beasts, and the unusual flavors made them an instant hit with children.

Rumors spread of strange ingredients coming from the dark corners of the Earth, of creatures, ancient artifacts, and powerful magics that went into the cakes. Some said Granny Weatherwax had made a deal with eldritch forces to ensure the success of her bakery.

Today, Granny Weatherwax and her granddaughters continue to run the Creepy Cakes bakery, providing birthday cakes for creepy children all over the world. The bakery's unique recipes, strange ingredients, and unusual designs keep the legacy of the Weatherwax family alive.

Granny has been baking since she was a young girl, and she has been running the Creepy Cakes bakery for over 50 years. Granny Weatherwax is truly a master of her craft, and her dedication and passion for baking have made Creepy Cakes one of the most beloved bakeries in town.
