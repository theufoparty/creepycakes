Our Products for creepy children.

White Tentacle Cake

This white tentacle horror lovecraftian birthday cake is a sight to behold. It’s a crafty confectionary creation ideal for those creepy children who like something a little off-kilter and mysterious to celebrate their special day. The entirety of the cake is iced in bright white buttercream and topped with realistic fondant tentacles that are waving across the top of the cake. The intricate details really add a stunning effect, resembling slimy sea creatures or other horrific Lovecraftian foes. These white tentacles double as decorative pieces too - as they swerve around each other and reach out from the main cake, in an eerie but captivating way.

Allowing for an unearthly ambiance at any birthday party or special occasion. In the middle of this tentacle structure lies another surprise; beneath a layer of blissful moist sponge cake lies raspberry jam stuffing, ideal if your child loves sweet treats! To finsh off the design, edible stars are sprinkled around the edges of the cake and small flaming candles affixed to a few of the tentacles, ready for that grand birthday moment where it’s time to make a wish. To bring this terrifying yet delightful pastry masterpeice home? Make sure you get enough for everyone: including any unfortunate monsters attending your event!
