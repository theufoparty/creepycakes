Our Products

For Creepy Children

From bloodlessly pale-skinned dolls to intricately piped scenes of terror, our creations promise to make a lasting impression. Cupcakes of all shapes and sizes, no less eldritch than our larger cakes, can be found adorning our shelves in a variety of flavors. From vanilla and chocolate with multiple shades of ghoulishly colored frosting options, to our signature black and red devil’s food, they are the perfect bite-size treats.

Whatever the occasion, our Creepy Cakes bakery will have something to suit your taste and put a bit of ghostly grandeur into the proceedings. Witches Cottage

A Spellbinding Treat

As one approaches a certain witches cottage on a moonlit night, amidst a barren landscape, one can behold a sinister looking confection of immense proportions. From afar, the cake looks to be made from a dark and devilish mixture of eerily hued concoctions, but as one draws nearer, one can detect the sweet aroma of its delectable flavors. The base of this cake is a hearty pumpkiny sponge, for the Witches House is known far and wide for its love of all things pumpkin.

Traversing the circumference of this cake is a thick and creamy ganache made with butterscotch and dark chocolate. An ornate sugary fence of caramelized marshmallows surrounds the perimeter, and lurking within this fence is a bed of chocolate and pistachio biscotti that offers texture and crunch. Topping off this cakish terror is a bitter dark chocolate frosting and a plethora of spooky decorations, from chocolate bats and eerie letters to pieces of chocolate-coated meringue. With such a devilishly delightful blend of flavors and decorations, it is no wonder why this cake is so popular at Witches House birthday celebrations.

Jaco-O-Lantern

A Taste of Hell

Gather ‘round children and hear the tale of a most delightful confection – the Jack-O'-Lantern Birthday Cake! This gruesome yet grandiose cake will be the centerpiece of any macabre affair and is sure to pique the curiosity of young and old alike! Its spooky visage is created with devilish detail. From the ghoulish face of the pumpkin, made of orange marzipan and ivory fondant, to the sinister black candles protruding from its mouth, this cake is certain to cast a spell over all who behold it.

The devil's grin, crafted with care and a hearty helping of chocolate ganache, speaks to the horror themes that lead to its wickedly wonderful concept. The cake itself is moist and flavorful, a veritable feast for the eyes and the palate. Filled with rich, creamy pumpkin buttercream and adorned with pumpkin-spice buttercream frosting, its delectable flavor is a sublime complement to its nightmarish design. One bite of this devilish delicacy is all it takes to fill the heart of any child with delight. And each time the birthday boy or girl blows out their Jack-O'-Lantern candles, their wish for a fearfully fun occasion shall be granted.

Midnight Octopus Delight

Deep Sea Delight

On this most singular of occasions, the birthday of one's nearest and dearest, one can take solace in a most peculiar and yet delightful gift: a delightful Black Tentacle Birthday Cake. This exquisite confectionary creation is sure to send a shiver of delight down to one's toes. The cake is crafted with an eye to the most discerning of tastes, fashioned in the shape of a wriggling, writhing tentacle of pure ebony, spiraling and looping, entwining a most intriguing design. Upon its surface is a layer of smooth chocolate frosting, fit to tempt the palate of any most discerning gourmand.

As one cuts into the cake, they will find within a macabre delight most dark and delectable. Bits of berry and cream, swirled and intermingled, to stir the appetite and shock the senses. Then, for the true wonder of the masterpiece, one must take a biting of the tentacle. As teeth break through its sugary crust, one must beware, for the tentacle will writhe and squirm in protest, wriggling and looping to the surprise and fright of all present. One can be assured to leave the celebration on a high, with a cake most delicious and unexpected!

Witches Brew

Bewitching Treat

Oh delicious cake, thy flavor is as a witches potion! Such a delight, this cake is made from unspeakable ingredients and frightful tastes. The Witches Brew birthday cake is a bewitching treat, with an almond flavored cake and an iced meringue topping. The frosting is an array of mystical flavors, combined to create a tantalizing sweet delight. The centerpiece of the cake is a fondant-coated topper. This accentuates the darkness of the dessert, a ghastly flavor to fit the theme.

The cake is a mix of flavors that arouse terror in the most intrepid of food connoisseurs. The almond cake is a nutty, dark flavor, while the icing is a blend of butter, sugar and a dab of cinnamon. Combined, these flavors invoke dread and create a sensation of horror in the eater. The Witches Brew Cake is a tempting treat that captures the essence of Victorian-era horror. For those who seek a devilishly delightful dessert, this cake is surely the best potion.

Eternal Celebrationt

Tomb Sweet Tomb

Ah, what a delightful and delightful treat for the occasion! Behold, the Gravestone birthday cake, a macabre and frightful sight to behold! The cake itself is made from the finest of ingredients, with a base of devil's food cake and a luxurious layer of coal-black fondant. Each of the gravestones is made of marzipan artfully carved to represent the dead who have come before.

The cake is layered in flavors of the dark side. The bottom layer is a dark chocolate mousse, topped by a layer of decadent dark cherry buttercream. The next layer is a layer of nutty peanut butter, followed by a mysterious and haunting layer of macadamia nut butterscotch. The top layer is filled with a delightful combination of light and dark chocolate frosting and a sprinkle of cocoa powder and sugar. It is a cake of many tastes and flavors, each one as bold and unique as the other. It is a fitting tribute for the dearly departed and a reminder of their enduring presence in our lives.

Eldritch Horror

Bring Out Your Inner Cthulhu

On this most special of days, a momentous event has been celebrated by presenting an Eldritch Horror birthday cake in honor of the blessed one. This fantastic confection is a sight to behold, a bewitching creation made of the most exquisite ingredients. The cake is filled with an oozy mélange of flavors, carefully chosen to befit such a remarkable occasion. A dark and eerie chocolate sponge forms the base, hinting of dark secrets yet to unfold. This is then layered with a lusciously creamy vanilla frosting, laced with a hint of spice to give the impression of mysterious powers lurking beneath.

The cake is then dusted with a sprinkling of mystical nuts, symbolizing the power of our hero’s journey. Finally, to make sure the occasion is truly unforgettable, the whole thing is adorned with a ghoulishly delightful array of eerie decorations. Taste and behold. This is a cake that will linger in the memory for many an odd year to come. Bon appétit!
